// About

Meet the Princess

Originally from Alaska, Lexi Lucks (also known as Alexandrea Bretz) is a trans docu-series personality, dancer, model, actress, and community builder based in Los Angeles. She first made national headlines on "Hooked on the Look" — featured as "Male Model Turned Beach Babe" — launching a media journey spanning 10+ countries.

She holds a Bachelor's in Multidisciplinary Studies (Dance & Business). After graduating, Lexi founded Lexi Lucks Productions, producing popup trans burlesque venues at nightclubs throughout LA and Orange County.

Crowned Miss West Hollywood USA and named one of LA Weekly's "Top 10 Women You Need to Know in 2023," Lexi now channels her energy into gaming, live streaming, cosplay, pageantry, and Transcend Foundation. Her biography is in progress.

// Community

Transcend Foundation

Founded by Lexi, the Transcend Foundation creates celebratory, affirming spaces for the LGBTQ+ community in LA — through events, fundraisers, and community-first experiences that center joy, identity, and belonging.

This builds on Lexi's roots producing trans burlesque shows through Lexi Lucks Productions — one of the first to put trans performers center stage at mainstream LA clubs. Transcend is that mission, scaled.

Community programming and advocacy are operated separately from Lexi Lucks creator partnerships. For nonprofit/community inquiries, use the Transcend contact path. For brand sponsorships and creator campaigns, use the Lexi Lucks collaboration path.

// Trans Advocacy

Behind the Crown

Behind the accolades and headlines lies a deeply scrutinized and frequently endangered community. Lexi is committed to raising the visibility and safety of trans individuals — not just celebrating wins, but naming the real dangers.

"Behind the accolades & headlines lies a very coveted and scrutinized subgroup of oppression and attacks on trans rights and individuals that only incentivizes nefarious acts of violence. Please give your kindness and support when you can to the trans community."

— Lexi Lucks

Trans Panic Defense — a legal mechanism allowing perpetrators to use a victim's trans identity as justification for violence — is one of the most dangerous laws Lexi advocates against.

// Pro Tips for Mass Production
📱 FILM VERTICAL ALWAYS
Always film in 9:16 portrait mode. One vertical video → TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts, Stories. One horizontal → Twitch clips, Twitter, YouTube. Never waste a shoot.
⚡ BATCH IN ONE OUTFIT
Film 10-15 clips in one outfit session. Upload all at once. AI generates unique captions for each. That's a month of content from one afternoon shoot.
🎯 HOOK IN FIRST 2 SECONDS
TikTok and Reels live or die in the first 2 seconds. Start mid-action, with a reaction, or with text overlay. The AI will write your hook — you just have to nail the opening shot.
💗 REPURPOSE EVERYTHING
One video → post to TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts, story highlights. One photo → Instagram post, Twitter, Facebook, Discord. The studio generates platform-specific copy for each.

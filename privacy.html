Privacy Policy

Effective April 28, 2026. This page explains how lexilucks.com collects and uses information for the Lexi Lucks creator business funnel.

Information Collected

Forms may collect name, email, phone number, Instagram handle, inquiry type, consent choices, referrer, landing page, and UTM campaign fields. The AI Concierge stores anonymous browser-local preferences so it can remember which site lane a visitor seems interested in.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used for Party Network updates, email/SMS reminders where consent is given, membership interest, brand collaboration inquiries, live-stream/community updates, and Transcend/community routing. Creator partnership inquiries and community/nonprofit inquiries are routed separately.

Analytics

The site includes a lightweight event layer that can connect to analytics providers such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or PostHog. Until those providers are connected, events are logged locally for testing.

SMS And Email

SMS messages are only intended for users who opt in. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out when SMS is connected. Email recipients can unsubscribe through the provider once email automation is connected.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@lexilucks.com.

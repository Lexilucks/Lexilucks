Questions Answered

Do I need to know how to code?▼
No. Everything is built in plain HTML files that you paste into GitHub (free) and it goes live automatically. The setup guide walks you through it step by step. If you can copy and paste, you can deploy this.
What hosting do I need?▼
GitHub Pages — it's completely free and what LexiLucks.com runs on. You get a free domain (yourname.github.io) or connect your own custom domain through Namecheap or GoDaddy for about $12/year. Zero monthly hosting costs.
How does the AI content studio work?▼
You set up a free Anthropic API key (Claude AI) once — takes 5 minutes. Then every time you hit "Generate" in the content studio, it writes captions in your exact voice for every platform you select. The AI system prompt is pre-written to match a creator brand — you just customize your name, titles, and personality details.
How do I take payments for my own subscriptions?▼
The monetization kit includes a detailed Stripe integration guide. Stripe is free to set up — you pay a small % only when you make a sale. Until you connect Stripe, form submissions route to your email via Formspree (also free). You're collecting leads from day one, converting them to paid members when you're ready.
Can I sell this to my own audience?▼
You're buying a personal license — you can use and customize these tools for yourself and up to 3 clients. If you want a reseller license to build and sell this as a service to unlimited clients, the bundle includes reseller rights. That's where the real money is.
Is there a refund policy?▼
30-day money back guarantee — no questions asked. If you deploy it and it doesn't work for you, email for a full refund. These are digital products so we ask that you give them a genuine attempt before requesting a refund.
How is this different from Linktree or Squarespace?▼
You own this. No monthly fee. No platform can ban you, change their terms, or take a cut of your membership revenue. Linktree is rented land. This is property. And it comes with an AI content engine that Squarespace will never give you.

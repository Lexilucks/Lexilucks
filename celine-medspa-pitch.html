The Three Phases

1
Initial Treatment
Celine Med Spa provides the CO₂ resurfacing and agreed treatments at a partial or full discount. Lexi documents the full consultation and treatment day — walk-through of the clinic, pre-treatment assessment, the procedure itself (as appropriate), and immediate post-care.
  • Clinic tour + provider introduction content
  • Pre-treatment consultation walkthrough
  • Day-of documentation (tasteful, educational)
  • Immediate aftercare instructions post
Low Clinic Risk · High Content Volume
2
Content Rollout + Performance Tracking
Content is released in timed waves over 3–6 months to align with the natural healing timeline of CO₂ (immediate → 1–3 months → peak results). Each post includes direct Celine Med Spa tag, geo-targeting for local audience, and trackable booking CTA.
  • Week 1–2: Healing documentary (high curiosity content)
  • Month 1: First results check-in
  • Month 3: Visible transformation reveal
  • Month 6: Final before/after comparison
  • Booking link or referral code tracked throughout
Timed Release · Trackable Results
3
Performance Unlock
Upon hitting agreed performance targets, remaining treatments (décolletage, temple, under-eye) are fully comped. Optional bonus content deliverables if performance exceeds targets.
